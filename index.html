Chief Fatai Disu Agboola

I am honored to be the first Akeweje of Ibeju-Lekki Kingdom, Lagos State, a prestigious title conferred upon me on March 23, 2013 by HRM Oba Rafiu Olusegun Salami, JP. This sacred chieftaincy title is not just a symbol of recognition but a deep responsibility to uphold the values, traditions, and progress of our great kingdom. The Akeweje title is reserved for individuals who have demonstrated outstanding leadership, unwavering commitment to community development, and a passion for preserving our cultural heritage.

My role as the Akeweje of Ibeju-Lekki extends beyond ceremonial duties. It is a call to serve, protect, and uplift my people. I have consistently worked towards promoting peace, fostering economic growth, and ensuring the preservation of our rich traditions while embracing modernization for sustainable development.

Guardian of Ibeju-Lekki: Head of the Vigilante Group

Security is the foundation of any thriving society, and I take great pride in my role as the Head of the Vigilante Group of Ibeju-Lekki. In a rapidly developing area like Ibeju-Lekki, security challenges are inevitable. However, my leadership in the local security sector has played a crucial role in safeguarding lives, properties, and businesses.

Under my command, the Vigilante Group has successfully:

  • Strengthened community policing initiatives.
  • Partnered with law enforcement agencies to combat crime.
  • Provided rapid response to security threats and emergencies.
  • Ensured peace and order, allowing residents and businesses to thrive.

I strongly believe that security is a collective effort, and I have actively engaged local stakeholders, traditional rulers, and the government to create a safer Ibeju-Lekki. Through strategic planning, intelligence gathering, and community collaboration, we have built a system that promotes vigilance, accountability, and safety.

Entrepreneurial Excellence: CEO of Golden Fasco Limited

Beyond my traditional and security roles, I am also a seasoned entrepreneur and business leader. As the CEO of Golden Fasco Limited, I have successfully built and managed a company that stands as a beacon of excellence and innovation in its industry.

Golden Fasco Limited was founded with the vision of creating sustainable business solutions, providing quality services, and driving economic growth. Through my leadership, the company has expanded its reach, created employment opportunities, and contributed to the commercial development of Lagos State. My entrepreneurial journey is fueled by my commitment to:

  • Innovation – Bringing fresh ideas and advanced solutions to business operations.
  • Integrity – Upholding the highest ethical standards in all dealings.
  • Empowerment – Creating opportunities for others to grow and succeed.

My Root & Commitment

My Roots and Heritage

My lineage is deeply rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Arapagi Fadunye, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos State, with ancestral ties to Arepo Arogunmasa Oguntolu Oniwonlu, Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos. Coming from a family with a deep connection to the traditions and leadership of Ibeju-Lekki, I have always been guided by the principles of honor, service, and progress.

My upbringing instilled in me a strong sense of responsibility to preserve the legacy of my ancestors while actively contributing to the growth and modernization of our community. I remain committed to bridging the gap between tradition and modernity, ensuring that our customs are not lost in the face of development but rather integrated into a thriving future.

Commitment to Service and Legacy

I firmly believe that leadership is not about titles but about impact and service. My life’s work has been dedicated to protecting, developing, and empowering my people through various means—whether in governance, security, business, or cultural preservation.

As the Akeweje of Ibeju-Lekki, Head of Security, and an Entrepreneur, I strive to:

  • ✔ Ensure the safety and security of our people and businesses.
  • ✔ Promote economic growth through sustainable business ventures.
  • ✔ Preserve our cultural heritage while embracing modern advancements.
  • ✔ Support community development initiatives that uplift the lives of our people.
  • ✔ Inspire and mentor the next generation of leaders and entrepreneurs.
